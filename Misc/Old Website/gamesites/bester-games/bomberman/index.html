Purpose
Destroy crates by exploding bombs near them
Fetch appearing powerups for extra strength
Touching explosions will make you lose a live!
Survive until you're the last one alive.

Keys
Player 1 (Red): Arrow keys + SHIFT
Player 2 (Blue): WASD + G
Player 3 (Purple): UHJK + L
Player 4 (Yellow): FCVB + N

Also..
Press Q to add a player in-game
Press P to remove a player in-game
Press ALT for suicide!
