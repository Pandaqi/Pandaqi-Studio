Purpose
Get as far as you can! You die if your coldness bar reaches 0.

Controls
Player 1: Arrow keys
> Left: Shoot icebolt. Can stop fires, disable rocket ice, and remove normal tiles
> Up: Jump, climb wall
> Down: Place normal tiles underneath yourself, release cloud

Player 2: WASD


Special Tiles
FIRE: Reduces your coldness
PARAPLU: Trampoline
ROCKET ICE: Takes you into the air for a few seconds
FRIDGE: Bump into it, it becomes a tunnel you can safely walk in
CLOUD: Hang in the air until it disappears/you let go
STICK: Skateboard until it disappears/you jump
