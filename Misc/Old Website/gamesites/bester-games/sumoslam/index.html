Purpose
Push all other players off the field by bumping into them!

Keys
Player 1 (Green): Arrow keys
Player 2 (Blue): WASD
Player 3 (Red): UHJK
Player 4 (Purple): FCVB

Extras
HUGE: Makes players tiny (or the field bigger)
OBSTACLES: Adds obstacles on the field to bump into
SPEEDY: Makes everyone much faster
SOCCER: Throw the rest off the field by bumping soccer balls into them
