Plot

This course is all about how to plot a story. How to pick events, situations and conflicts that will bring out the best from your characters and setting. If you’re just starting out, first read my general course on Storytelling. It links to this more specific course whenever needed.

I am a professional author under my real name: Tiamo Pastoor. While that’s no proof of expertise, hopefully it convinces you to give my course a try! As always, I aim to be practical, fun and efficient when teaching.
