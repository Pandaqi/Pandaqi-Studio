Swiftsmash Saga 👋

🥐 Breadcrumbs || Home / Blog / Boardgames / Swiftsmash saga

The Swiftsmash Saga is a collection of games about being the first to smash (or just tap/grab/get) the right or wrong card. They’re simple party games about quick-thinking and dexterity, where the rules about what to smash (or when) constantly change, and you have to keep up or bend them in your favor.
