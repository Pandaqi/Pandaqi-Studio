Swiftsmash Saga

The Swiftsmash Saga is a collection of games about being the first to smash (or just tap/grab/get) the right or wrong card. They’re simple party games about quick-thinking and dexterity, where the rules about what to smash (or when) constantly change, and you have to keep up or bend them in your favor.

The Outnumbered Series

The Outnumbered Series are a collection of simple card games that all revolve around one unique number. For example, in Nine Lives, you start with … nine lives and must not be the first to lose them all. They all provide a simple twist on how to deal with that number and either use it or lose it, creating math-based card games for the whole family.
