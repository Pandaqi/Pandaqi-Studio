{{% section-centered heading="What's this about?" %}} All players play the head of a thieving crew, obsessed with stealing treasures and then fleeing the city. Coincidentally, they also win the game if they do that. On your turn, take one of these actions: - **Move yourself:** if you end on a treasure spot, you grab it. But you can't go past police or other players. - **Move police:** move the police through the city. Each treasure gives you special powers. But the police might also have some special powers ... A game about stealing the most useful treasures before anyone else, setting yourself up for a quick exit, and making sure your fellow players don't completely block all your passages. {{% /section-centered %}}
